What is Mumble?

Mumble is a fast-paced card game that blends creativity, wordplay, and unpredictability. Players must speak, shout, or stay silent based on the cards they draw. It’s hilarious, weird, and perfect for groups.

How to Play

  1. Each player gets 1 Word Card, 1 Task Card and 3 Power Cards.
  2. The players should start a conversation, and tries to complete their tasks.
  3. Some cards challenge your speech, others your actions.
  4. The players who finish their tasks wins!

Game Rules

  • No skipping challenges — embrace the chaos!
  • Speak only when allowed by the card rules.
  • If you break a rule, draw an extra card.
  • Some cards may team you up with others — work together!
