What is Mumble?

Mumble is a fast-paced card game that blends creativity, wordplay, and unpredictability. Players must speak, shout, or stay silent based on the cards they draw. It’s hilarious, weird, and perfect for groups.

How to Play

  1. Each player gets 5 cards.
  2. On your turn, draw and follow the instructions.
  3. Speak, act, or stay silent — the cards decide!
  4. First to discard all cards wins!

Game Rules

  • No skipping challenges — embrace the chaos!
  • If you mess up, draw a penalty card.
  • Collaboration and sabotage are allowed!
