What is Mumble?

Mumble is a fast-paced, unpredictable card game where players compete in challenges, complete secret tasks, and navigate rule-twisting chaos — all while trying to keep a straight face.

How to Play

  1. Get your Word, Task, and Power Cards.
  2. Start talking — try to complete your secret task.
  3. Play Power Cards to mess with opponents.
  4. First to complete the task wins. Easy? Think again.

Game Rules

  • Follow card instructions exactly.
  • Breaking a rule? Draw an extra card.
  • Stay in character when required — no excuses!
  • Team-up cards flip the game upside down.
